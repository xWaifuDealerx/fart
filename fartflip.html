How it works. You sign a transferChecked instruction sending your bet to the FartFlip treasury wallet. The server cryptographically flips the coin (Node crypto.randomInt) and signs a 2× payout transaction back to your wallet if you win. Every bet is single-use (signature dedupe). Lose and your tokens stay in the treasury — they fund future winners. The treasury wallet is set up by the admin and needs to hold enough $FARTPRINT to cover any potential 2× payout.
