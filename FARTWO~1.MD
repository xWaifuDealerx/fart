# FartWorld — Engagement & Retention Playbook

A working list of features designed to hook players, lengthen sessions, and push word-of-mouth growth. Grouped by motivation type and ordered roughly from cheapest-to-build → biggest-payoff.

---

## 1. Compulsion loops (daily return)

**Daily Spin / Loot Wheel** — once per 24 hours, spin a roulette in the Hotel lobby for silver, ammo, rare jars, or a chance at a Rainbow Fart Jar. Streak multipliers stack with the existing login streak.

**Mailbox at the Hotel** — letters arrive while offline: gifts from Carlos, complaints from cats, hate mail from junkies. Reading them grants tiny rewards and seeds story.

**Limited 24h NPC moods** — Carlos discounts 15 % on Tuesdays, Wave runs a yacht sale on Fridays, Gary pays +50 % on Sundays. Pushed to the HUD on login.

**Daily quest board at the bank** — three random tasks ("Sell 5 Fart Jars", "Mine 10 Iron", "Survive a spider wave in the hotel"). Completion = silver + XP, with a weekly meta-reward for hitting all 21.

---

## 2. Status & progression hooks

**Achievement gallery in your house** — busts, trophies, and silver plaques unlock as you hit milestones. Visiting another player's house should make them flex.

**Cosmetic printer skins** — paint your printer, swap antennas, add LEDs. Earned through gameplay or buyable with Gold. Wearing a flag already exists; lean into more wearables (sunglasses, crowns, fart auras).

**Prestige system** — at Lv 50 you can prestige back to Lv 1 in exchange for permanent +5 % silver gains and a "★" beside your name on the leaderboard. Repeat infinitely with diminishing but stacking returns.

**Public titles** — "Spider Slayer", "World Cup Holder (FARTJPN)", "30-Day Streak". Shown on chat bubbles + leaderboard.

---

## 3. PvP & social pressure

**Bounty board at Gary's** — players can place a bounty (silver) on someone's head. The hunter shoots them in a designated PvP zone and collects.

**Heist mode** — once per day a vault appears at the bank. 2-6 players cooperate to crack it; rewards split based on contribution. Cops (NPC) chase you out.

**Plot raids** — at level 20+ a player can spend silver to "investigate" another player's plot, stealing 5 % of stored crops. Defender gets a counter-raid window the next day.

**Live duels in the arena with side bets** — spectators can bet silver on the outcome. Winner's pot grows, gambling adds a watch-loop reason to hang out.

**Photo mode + global feed** — F11 takes a styled screenshot with your handle watermarked. Auto-shares to a `/news` feed inside the index site. Top photos win silver.

---

## 4. Economy & speculation

**Live $FARTPRINT price chart in the bank** — react to real on-chain prices. A 5 % daily move triggers a "PUMP!" or "DUMP!" floater across all online players.

**Player-set market prices** — let players list items at their own price. Carlos becomes a price floor, but listings can undercut him. Adds emergent economics + arbitrage.

**Land deeds** — finite numbered plot NFTs (off-chain at first). Rarer plots near the dock or arena go for huge silver / Gold. Owners earn a tiny tax on every transaction made on their plot.

**Hotel ownership** — at endgame, top players bid to OWN the Hotel for a week. Owner earns 10 % of every booking until the next bid round.

**Insurance** — pay 20 % premium up-front to keep your inventory if you get busted with fake cash or die in PvP. Adds risk dial-up gameplay.

---

## 5. World events (FOMO)

**Solar Eclipse** (monthly) — sky goes dark for 30 minutes. Spiders triple-spawn, but every kill drops a Mythic Coin. Trade 10 to Alexandre for a unique skin.

**Fart Cup Match Days** — when there's a real World Cup match, the winning country's token holders earn an automatic silver dividend. Drives token holding behaviour.

**Black market caravan** — once a week, a hooded printer caravan parks somewhere random for 10 minutes selling banned items (counterfeit ink stamps, weed boosters, jar accelerators).

**Server-wide goals** — "Print 1 million FART Jars this week" → unlocks a permanent cosmetic for everyone who contributed.

---

## 6. Onboarding & retention

**Tutorial polish** — already in. Add a "Skip ahead" for returning players, and a "Tutorial complete" cosmetic badge (small crown next to handle).

**5-minute first session win** — give every new player a free Fart Jar on first login so they have something to sell immediately. Triggers the dopamine hit fast.

**Friend referral upgrade** — current code: invitee gives inviter 1 invite. Upgrade: when invitee hits Lv 10, both get a one-time bonus (5 K silver). Makes invitations actually meaningful.

**Push notifications** — opt-in browser push when crops are ready, hotel booking expires soon, bounty placed on you, friend joined the game.

**Resume-friendly UI** — on login, popup the player to ask "Welcome back. You were last seen at the [Hospital] with [120 silver]. Want to fast-travel?". Removes the cold-start tax.

---

## 7. Multiplayer hooks

**Co-op spider waves** — a "Defend the Hotel" mode where up to 5 players hold the lobby against a swarm. Pays based on survival time + spiders killed.

**Voice rooms in buildings** — opt-in WebRTC voice chat inside the bank, market, or hotel lobby. Public bar feel.

**Guilds / printer cartels** — form a 10-person group. Shared bank, group quests, group leaderboard, custom emblem on your name tag.

**Live world map on the index page** — show real online players as moving dots so the homepage feels alive. Click any dot → spectate that player for 30 seconds.

---

## 8. Meta / out-of-game

**Discord bot** — `/myfart` shows your current silver/level. `/leaderboard` posts current top 10. Spider-kill streaks auto-post a flex.

**X integration** — already has share-on-X for milestones. Add a "Photo of the Day" auto-tweet from the in-game photo mode.

**TikTok highlights** — record a 6-second clip of your best fart explosion → one-click upload watermarked with @FARTPRINT handle.

**On-chain marketplace** — turn rare items (Rainbow Fart Jars, Hotel ownership weeks) into actual SPL tokens. NFT scarcity drives speculation.

---

## Suggested first-three to ship

1. **Daily spin wheel** in the Hotel lobby (low cost, huge return-rate lift).
2. **Bounty board at Gary's** (zero new infra needed, drives PvP).
3. **5-minute first-session win** (free starter jar) — fixes the new-player drop-off.

These three together would move 7-day retention more than any individual content drop.
