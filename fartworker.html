How it works

The grant flow

  1. Apply. Connect Phantom, enter your X handle, write a short pitch for what you'd contribute. Sign the application with your wallet to prove it's yours.
  2. Review. Every submission appears in the public queue. The protocol stewards review and decide whether to accept, what role you fit, and what amount you'll be sent.
  3. Stake & receive. Accepted grants are staked via Printr on your behalf, locked for the agreed period (≤180 days). You earn fee revenue on the stake from day one.
  4. Unstake. When the lock period ends you can withdraw the principal or roll into a new stake. Fees you earn during the lock are yours to claim.
No guarantees. Applying does not guarantee acceptance, an amount, or a role. The protocol can decline any submission for any reason or no reason at all. There is no obligation to act on, respond to, or hold any application. This is not employment, a security, or a promise of return.
