Getting Started

You spawn on a green fantasy island as a sentient printer. Move with WASD, look around by dragging the mouse, scroll to zoom, press SPACE to fart, press E to interact.

First moves

  • Follow the 5-step tutorial top-right: steal Gary's bike, melt sand into jars, fart in them, sell to Gary. Each step pays +50 silver, full completion pays +200 bonus.
  • Press I for inventory, P for portfolio, click 🏆 top-right for the worldwide leaderboard.
  • Visit the Bank (-22, -8) to swap silver / cash / gold.
  • Mine ore with a pickaxe, fart in jars, plant weed, run hustles in the Data Center.
  • When you die or get busted, you respawn at the Hospital (-64, -8). Heal for 30 silver.

Controls

  • WASD - move · SHIFT - run · SPACE - fart / jump
  • E - interact with NPCs, buildings, vehicles
  • F - use equipped tool (pickaxe swing)
  • G - scoop sand on the beach
  • B - mount / eject bike
  • M - fullscreen minimap · L - leaderboard
  • Left-click - fire Desert Eagle (when owned)

Currencies & Conversion

FartWorld has a three-tier currency system, plus optional crypto:

  • 🥈 Silver - main in-game currency. Earn from quests, mining, NPC trades.
  • 💵 Cash - high-volume currency. 1 silver = 50 cash (rate inflates +0.1/day from base 50). Used for printer paper, food, NPC trades.
  • 🪙 Fake Cash - counterfeit. Sometimes accepted, 50% chance of getting busted by cops and going to jail for 5 minutes.
  • 🥇 Gold - premium currency. Buy from the Bank for ~100,887 silver per gold (rate inflates +1 silver/day from base 100,000). Backed by burned $FARTPRINT.

Bank conversions

  • Silver → Cash · 1 → 50 (forward rate)
  • Cash → Silver · 100 → 1 (buyback rate; 50% spread is the inflation cost)
  • Silver → Gold · ~100K silver per 1 gold, fractional purchases supported
  • Gold → Silver · 1 gold → ~100K silver at current rate

Resources

Ores (pickaxe required)

  • 🟫 Copper · cheapest, easiest
  • ⬜ Tin · light, common
  • 🔘 Iron · staple ore
  • 🩶 Titanium · valuable
  • 🌑 Adamantium · rare
  • 💎 Mithril · top tier · trades highest at Miner's Exchange

Sell only silver-paying ores at the Miner's Exchange (Traech). Tool durability drops 8% per hit; sharpen at Tool Service (Siim).

Crafting line

  • 🛍 Plastic Bag (from Carlos) → 🏖 Bag of Sand (G on beach) → 🫙 Empty Jar (Glassworks E) → 🫙 Fart Jar (FFS, 1 of 5 tiers)
  • Jar tiers (rarity): 🟢 Green (60%) · 🔵 Blue (25%) · 🟣 Purple (10%) · 🟠 Orange (4%) · 🌈 Rainbow (1%)

Farming

  • Buy a Plot from Carlos. Plant weed seeds. Wait the timer. Roll a tier when grown.
  • Weed tiers same as jars. Sell to Carlos or the Pawn.
  • Carrots can be sold to Roki at a premium.

Wood / Paper

  • Chop trees with a saw. Mill wood at the Paper Mill into paper. Paper can be laundered upstairs in the house.

Mechanics

Health Bar

100 HP cap. Fall damage from drops > 4m. Spider bite = 8 HP per cycle. Passive regen = +1 HP / 30s. Hospital = full heal for 30🥈. Death = wake at Hospital with full HP (inventory preserved unless busted).

Bust Risk

Paying with Fake Cash carries a 50% chance of getting busted. Bust = 5 minutes in Jail, inventory wiped (except wearables / vault). Vault contents are always safe.

Day / Night

24-hour cycle compressed in-game. Streetlights glow at night. The world is darker, mining is slower.

Hotel / Sleep / AFK

Pay 25🥈/hour at the Hotel for a safe-zone bed up to 72 hours. Press E inside to sleep - movement locks, spiders can't reach you. Press E again to wake up.

Apartments (Own + Rent Out)

Once you buy an apartment it's yours forever. Sleep in it for free. Rent it out 1–365 days for daily passive silver income. Soviet 800🥈/day, Middle 2,400🥈/day, Luxury 12,000🥈/day.

Mining Animation

Hold F next to an ore. The progress bar fills based on pickaxe durability (1×–2.5× slower at low durability). Yield chance scales with durability. Sharpen at Tool Service to keep speed up.

Rocket Ship

Unlocks at Level 30. Board at the pad (24, -22), launch with SPACE, walk on the moon, return.

Referrals

Every player has a referral code. Invitees who use your link earn YOU 1% of every silver they collect, forever. Manage your referrals from the 🤝 button top-right.

The (Optional) Crypto Layer

You can play the game without ever touching crypto. But for those who want to:

$FARTPRINT (the engine)

  • The native Solana token of the ecosystem.
  • Every Gold purchase in FartWorld is settled in $FARTPRINT and burns it from circulation.
  • Hold the token and stake at printr.money to receive your share of trading fees.

Fart Cup Country Tokens

  • Real pump.fun tokens, one per World Cup country (FARTAUS, FARTGER, FARTIRN, etc.).
  • Hold ≥100,000 of a country's token to wear its flag as a Fungible Non-Fungible Token (FNFT) - no mint, no claim, your wallet balance IS the NFT.
  • When the World Cup is live, real match wins pay silver bonuses to wallets holding the winning team's tokens.
  • All trading fees from every Fart Cup country token are routed back to buy back and burn $FARTPRINT - every trade tightens the supply.

FART&POOP500

  • A basket-style token system - trade them at the Poop House.
  • Holdings yield in-game silver drips while the game is running.
  • All trading fees on FART&POOP500 tokens also flow back into the same $FARTPRINT buy-back and burn - so every Fart Cup and FART&POOP500 trade makes $FARTPRINT scarcer.

Wearables

  • Flags worn in-game are FNFTs (your wallet's holding IS the wearable).
  • Future planned: skins, hats, accessories tied to on-chain ownership.
